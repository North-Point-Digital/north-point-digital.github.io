# Commercial content plan — closing the high-intent gap

_June 2026. Companion to docs/redesign-spec.md. Queries validated against live
SERPs; no keyword-volume tool was connected, so volumes are directional — connect
Ahrefs/GSC data to refine priorities after the first quarter._

## Why these posts

The existing twelve posts cover explainers (AI-Overview fodder, low clicks) and
decision pieces (good mid-funnel). What's missing is **commercial intent**: cost,
comparison and hiring queries where the searcher is close to spending. The SERPs
for these queries are currently held by individual consultants' thin cost guides
(helium42, daveknowsai, nicolalazzari, lilachbullock) — beatable with properly
structured, sourced content on a real consultancy domain.

Cadence assumption: one post a week. Bottom-funnel first.

## Rules for every post

- Lead with the answer (price bands, the verdict) in the first screen — these
  queries get AI-Overview'd; the post must be the best citation.
- Concrete numbers with sources; UK English; ban list applies (see
  docs/redesign-spec.md §3).
- One soft CTA at the end, linking the relevant offer page + free call.
- Category: "AI Strategy" unless clearly "AWS & Cloud".
- After publishing: two LinkedIn derivatives per post (one stat-led, one
  opinion-led).

## Measurement

- Google Search Console: check monthly which queries get impressions; write the
  next comparison/cost post against whatever clusters.
- GA4 `cta_click` labels per post footer already wired.
- Success marker for the quarter: any post ranking page 1 for a cost/comparison
  query, and the playbook conversion rate from blog traffic.
